Project: New E-commerce Platform

**1. Introduction**
We are a retail company looking to build a new e-commerce platform to expand our online presence.

**2. Scope of Work**
The project will involve the following:
- Design and development of a new website.
- Integration with our existing inventory management system.
- A secure payment gateway.
- A content management system (CMS) for managing products and promotions.

**3. Technical Requirements**
- The platform should be built using a modern technology stack (e.g., Python, Django, React).
- It must be scalable to handle up to 1 million users.
- It must be compliant with PCI DSS for payment processing.

**4. Timeline**
The project should be completed within 6 months.

**5. Budget**
The budget for this project is $250,000.
